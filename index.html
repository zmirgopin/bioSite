All About

Soshie Gopin

Between balancing marriage, raising six children, and a professional career as a purchasing coordinator, Soshie Gopin, is a busy lady. Soshie’s goal in life is to be a role model to her children through her actions, but also to teach them to behave with honesty, and integrity, and to be good members of society. Her parents and grandparents, or her role models, taught these lessons to her and she hopes to pass them on to her children. Soshie also tries to be a positive person, especially while around her kids. She says, “I try to teach lessons of judging people favorably, talking nicely towards others, and that everything that happens in our life is G-d’s will and exactly what is meant to be”.
